Our Story

Tikim Heaven began with a dream: to preserve and share the rich culinary traditions of the Philippines with the world. Founded by a passionate foodie who grew up savoring the bold and comforting flavors of Filipino dishes, Tikim Heaven is more than just a collection of recipes—it’s a celebration of heritage, family, and community.

Every recipe featured here tells a story of culture and love passed down through generations. From bustling market days to Sunday family feasts, our dishes connect us to the heart of Filipino life.

Join us in this journey of taste and tradition. Together, let's honor the flavors that make the Philippines truly special. Tara, tikim tayo!
